    About Me

    Hello, my name is Oluwasola. My journey in data analytics started when I first collected hospital data with the aim of investigating the prevalence of sickle cell disease in Katsina, Nigeria during my NYSC program. With the support of well-meaning stakeholders, the project soon led to an initiative that educated over 7000 locals on how to mitigate the identified risks.

    In my current role as a Biomedical scientist, I have harnessed data analytics to solve problems associated with quality control, operations, inventory problems and as well as improving the turn-around-time by 85% through analyzing daily workflow data.

    Although I have been around the tech space for a while teaching myself how to create things for the web, the successes I have recorded using data outweigh that of any other career path. Sincerely, I am intrigued by those achievements. They are my motivation to continue along this path as long as it lasts.
